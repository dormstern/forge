✗ KILL — Cope Moat                                  conf: 92
  "GPT-4 Vision does the OCR" is not a moat. Every
  competitor has the same access. You're a UI on top of
  somebody else's API. Six weeks until someone wraps the
  same call in a $9/mo app and undercuts you.
  Cheap test (≤48h): name 3 categories of receipts your
  product handles that Expensify+Vision wrappers cannot.

✗ KILL — False Champion                              conf: 87
  "Small business owners want this" is a bystander, not a
  buyer. Name the specific person — title + company size +
  what they're using today + what would have to be true for
  them to switch. If you can't, you have hope, not a champion.
  Cheap test: text 3 actual SMB owners and ask "what do you
  use for receipts today, and why?"

✗ KILL — TAM Mirage                                  conf: 84
  "$50B expense management market" is the headline number,
  not your TAM. Your TAM is "SMBs who hate Expensify enough
  to switch + would pay $20-50/mo + can be reached via
  channels you can afford." That's probably <$200M, before
  the GPT-4 wrappers split it.

⚠ WOUND — Timeline Cope                              conf: 76
  Your roadmap quotes "MVP in 4 weeks." Receipt OCR is the
  easy part — the hard part is multi-tenant accounts, OAuth
  with QuickBooks/Xero, audit trails for tax season, refund/
  expense reconciliation. Realistic: 12 weeks to anything
  defensible. Adjust expectations or pre-pick which 2 of
  those 4 you're skipping.

⚠ WOUND — Weak Wedge                                 conf: 71
  "Better receipt OCR" is a feature wedge. Your wedge needs
  to be a *workflow* wedge: which specific moment in the
  expense-management workflow does ReceiptScan replace,
  end-to-end, in a way Expensify can't catch up to in 6 months?

⚠ WOUND — Replicability Risk                         conf: 68
  Three competitors are 30 days behind you on the same idea
  (Mindee, Veryfi, Nanonets all have receipt-OCR APIs you
  could be wrapping). If your only moat is "we shipped
  first," you'll be re-platformed within a year.

⚠ WOUND — Distribution Gap                           conf: 64
  How does an SMB owner find you? "SEO + content marketing"
  is what every wrapper says. Real distribution requires
  partnerships (QuickBooks app marketplace, Xero, FreshBooks)
  or a hand-built channel (accountant networks). Pick one
  and budget 6 months.

✓ SURVIVE — Cherry-Picking                           conf: 89
  Your plan included 2 contradicting signals (Mindee's
  $3M/yr revenue ceiling on the same wedge, and the
  Expensify pricing-floor problem). You're not lying to
  yourself about the data. That's rare and worth keeping.
